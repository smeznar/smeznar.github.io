I am currently a master's degree student at the University of Ljubljana, Faculty of mathematics and physics. My field of study is computer science and mathematics, but I specialize in machine learning and data science. I also work as a student researcher at the Jožef Stefan Institute, Department of Knowledge Technologies.

Interests
------
My academic interests lie primarily in machine learning (mostly interpretable), computer vision, and
data science. Lately, I have been mostly working with graphs. This sparked a strong interest since
graphs can contain much more information than normal tabular data, are (in my opinion) underused, and
understudied.

In my free time I like to watch and play sports, mostly volleyball and football (soccer). My biggest achievement in volleyball is the title of national under-18 champions with GO Volley in 2013/14 season. I also like reading, especially epic fantasy like for example the cosmere universe.
